Punch Electric Vehicle

The Tata Punch EV is a compact, all-electric SUV with a range of 315–421 km. It has a stylish design, a spacious interior, and a variety of modern features. Here are some of its features and specifications:
Design: The Punch EV has a front trunk (frunk) and EV-specific styling.
Range: The Punch EV has a range of 315–421 km.
Charging: The Punch EV can be charged using a 3.3 kW AC charger box, 7.2 kW AC fast charger, or DC fast charger.
Safety: The Punch EV has a number of safety features, including ABS, EBD, hill hold assist, ISOFIX, iTPMS, and rear parking sensor.
Comfort and convenience: The Punch EV has a number of comfort and convenience features, including a digital cockpit, air purifier, cruise control, and 360-degree surround view camera system.
Variants: The Punch EV is available in 20 variants, with the Smart being the cheapest and the Empowered Plus S LR AC FC being the most expensive.
Motor: The Punch EV has a permanent magnet synchronous motor (PMSM) with a motor power of 60 kW and a max torque of 114 Nm.
